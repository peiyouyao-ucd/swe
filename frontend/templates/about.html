Our Story

DublinBikes was launched in 2009 as a pioneering bike-sharing initiative to transform urban mobility in Dublin. What started with 40 stations has grown into a comprehensive network serving the entire city.

Since our launch, we've helped thousands of people discover the joy of cycling while reducing traffic congestion and promoting a healthier, more sustainable way to explore our beautiful city.

With over 110 stations across Dublin and a fleet of well-maintained bikes, we're committed to providing a reliable, eco-friendly transportation solution that fits your lifestyle. Our AI-powered systems now help cyclists plan better routes and make informed decisions about when and where to ride.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at DublinBikes

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing carbon emissions and promoting eco-friendly transportation throughout Dublin.

Health & Wellness

Encouraging active lifestyles and making it easier for Dubliners to incorporate exercise into their daily routine.

Making cycling accessible to everyone with affordable pricing and strategically located stations across the city.

Regular bike maintenance, safety guidelines, and support to ensure a safe cycling experience for all users.
