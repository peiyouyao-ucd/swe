How to Rent a Bike

Renting a bike is simple and quick

1

Find a Station

Use the DublinBikes app or website to locate the nearest bike station with available bikes. Our real-time map shows bike availability at all 110+ stations.

Live station data updated every 30 seconds
2

Select Your Bike

Check the bike's condition before renting. Look for any visible damage and ensure the tires are properly inflated and brakes work correctly.

3

Unlock the Bike

Open the DublinBikes app and scan the QR code on the bike, or enter the bike number manually. The bike will unlock automatically within seconds.

QR code located on the handlebars
4

Start Your Ride

Once unlocked, remove the bike from the dock. Adjust the seat height if needed and start your journey. Your ride timer begins automatically.

First Time Renting?

  • Make sure you have the DublinBikes app installed and an active account
  • Add a valid payment method to your account before renting
  • Check your phone has a stable internet connection
  • Choose a subscription plan or use pay-as-you-go option

How to Use the Service

Making the most of your DublinBikes experience

Mobile App Features

  • Real-time Map: See all stations and bike availability
  • Route Planning: Get directions to your destination
  • Ride History: Track all your rides and statistics
  • Weather Forecast: AI-powered cycling conditions
  • Station Alerts: Get notified about station status

Safety Guidelines

  • Always wear a helmet (not provided, bring your own)
  • Follow all traffic rules and signals
  • Use bike lanes where available
  • Turn on lights in low visibility conditions
  • Stay visible to other road users

Ride Duration

30-Minute Rides: All subscription plans include unlimited 30-minute rides at no extra cost.

Extended Rides: Rides over 30 minutes incur additional charges (€0.50 per 30 minutes).

Pro Tip: Return your bike to any station before 30 minutes, then rent again to avoid extra charges!

Active ride timer visible in app

Subscription Plans

  • Pay As You Go: €2 per ride (30 minutes)
  • Weekly Pass: €15 for unlimited 30-min rides
  • Monthly Pass: €35 for unlimited 30-min rides
  • Annual Pass: €120 for unlimited 30-min rides (Save 60%!)
Change plans anytime in the app

Important Reminders

  • Bikes are for personal use only - no passengers allowed
  • Do not leave your bike unattended outside a station
  • Report any damage or issues immediately through the app
  • Lock your bike if you need to make a quick stop
  • You are responsible for the bike until it's properly returned

How to Return the Bike

Returning your bike safely and correctly

1

Find an Available Dock

Use the app to find nearby stations with free docking spaces. The map shows real-time availability at each station.

Filter by stations with available docks
2

Align the Bike

Push the bike firmly into an empty dock. Make sure the front wheel is properly aligned with the docking mechanism.

3

Wait for Confirmation

The dock will beep and show a green light when the bike is securely locked. Wait for this confirmation before leaving.

Green light = Successfully returned
4

Check Your App

Your ride will automatically end and you'll receive a summary showing ride duration, distance, and any charges.

What if all docks are full?

Option 1: The app will show you the nearest station with available docks.

Option 2: Press the call button on the station terminal for 15 extra minutes of free riding time.

Option 3: Contact customer support through the app for immediate assistance.

Return Checklist

  • Bike is pushed firmly into the dock
  • Green light is displayed on dock
  • Confirmation beep heard
  • App shows ride has ended
  • No items left on the bike

Remember

You remain responsible for the bike until you receive confirmation that it has been properly returned. Always double-check before leaving the station!

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How do I create an account?

Download the DublinBikes app from the App Store or Google Play, click "Sign Up," and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide your email, create a password, add a payment method, and verify your phone number.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), as well as PayPal and Apple Pay. Payment methods can be managed in your account settings.

Can I rent multiple bikes at once?

No, each account can only have one active rental at a time. If you need to rent bikes for a group, each person will need their own account and subscription.

What happens if the bike is damaged?

If you notice damage before renting, report it in the app and choose a different bike. If damage occurs during your ride, contact customer support immediately.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through the app settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial months.

What if I forget to return the bike?

The bike remains your responsibility until properly returned to a station. Extended rental fees will continue to accrue. If a bike is not returned within 24 hours, it may be reported as stolen and you'll be charged the full replacement cost (€1,200).

Are helmets provided?

No, helmets are not provided with our bikes. We strongly recommend bringing your own helmet for safety. Helmets are legally required for cyclists under 18 in Ireland.

Can I use DublinBikes outside of Dublin?

No, DublinBikes must be returned to a station within the Dublin service area. Taking bikes outside this area violates our terms of service and may result in additional fees and account suspension.

What should I do if I have an accident?

First, ensure you're safe and seek medical attention if needed. Then, report the incident to local authorities if required, and contact DublinBikes customer support as soon as possible. Document the scene with photos if safe to do so.

How do I report a problem with a bike or station?

You can report issues directly through the app by selecting the bike or station and clicking "Report Problem." Our maintenance team will be notified immediately and will address the issue as quickly as possible.

Is there a student discount?

Yes! Students with a valid student ID can get 20% off annual subscriptions. Verify your student status through the app to access this discount.

Can I reserve a bike in advance?

Bikes cannot be reserved in advance. Our system is designed for immediate, real-time rentals. The app shows live availability at all stations to help you plan your journey.

What if my phone battery dies during a ride?

The bike will remain unlocked and your ride will continue. However, you won't be able to see station availability for return. We recommend carrying a portable charger or planning your route in advance.

Are the bikes electric?

Currently, our fleet consists of traditional pedal bikes. We are piloting electric bikes at select stations and plan to expand our e-bike offering in 2026.

How often are bikes maintained?

All bikes undergo regular maintenance checks weekly. Our maintenance team also responds to reported issues within 24 hours. Bikes are thoroughly serviced monthly and replaced every 3-5 years.

Still have questions?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to help you.
